About Us

ABC Billiards Club was founded in 1972 by a group of casual players looking to expand their horizons and meet new people who shared a love for the game. Interest was phenomenal and by 1978 we were able to find a permanent home for the club on Main Street. The missions of ABC Billiards Club are to provide a welcoming location for games and offer members the opportunity to challenge themselves in competitive play. Everyone is welcomed to visit our facilities and see what we have to offer!
